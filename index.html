Welcome!

This simple guide is going to walk you through the basics of what you need to know to play Dungeons & Dragons.

There are a lot of resources, rule books, guides, and other information about this game on the internet, and it can be very overwhelming to take in all at once, or to even know how where to start.

The aim of this guide is to shave down some of the information that you don't need to know right away and set you up with an entry-level amount of knowledge that you can build on as you play the game!

We're going to go discuss some of the core mechanics of the game, as well as some details about creating a character. At the end of the guide, you'll also have the option to create your first character based on what you learned!

If you're ready, let's get started!
